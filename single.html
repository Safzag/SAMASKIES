Seek mental health

Sometimes, stepping back from technology is just what we need for our mental health. It's easy to get caught up in screens, social media, and constant notifications, but all that can leave us feeling drained and stressed. Taking a break-even just putting the phone down for a bit—can help clear our minds and bring a sense of calm. It gives us a chance to breathe, connect with the world around us, and recharge. Making time to unplug is a simple way to take care of ourselves and feel more balanced.
